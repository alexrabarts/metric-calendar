The Philosophy

The seven days need no collective name because they need no collective purpose. Primday, Duoday, Triday... they're just days — unstructured time to spend however you please.

Only the pause is named: The Rest.

"The Rest" carries a double meaning: restoration and stillness, but also "the remainder." It doesn't pick a side between doing and not-doing.
